✦ What is Numerology?

The Ancient Science
of Numbers & Destiny

Numerology is one of the world's oldest mystical sciences — dating back over 4,000 years to ancient Babylon, Egypt, and Greece. It is the belief that numbers are the universal language through which the cosmos communicates with us. Every number vibrates at a unique frequency that influences personality, purpose, timing, and life events.
